# Naming

## Selected Name

`ClusterOrbit`

## Why It Fits

- `Cluster` is direct and grounded in the product domain.
- `Orbit` implies spatial awareness, movement, and understanding relationships over time.
- The combined name supports the product's topology-first identity without sounding toy-like.

## Rejected Alternatives

- `TopoForge`: strong, but more abstract.
- `OpsAtlas`: useful, but less tied to clusters specifically.
- `MeshPilot`: good distributed-systems tone, but less centered on machine layout.
