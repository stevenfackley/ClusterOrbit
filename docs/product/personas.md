# Personas

## Homelab Operator

Runs k3s or lightweight Kubernetes on local hardware, edge devices, or mini PCs. Values fast setup, direct `kubeconfig` access, and a visual way to understand node and service placement.

## Platform Engineer

Manages multiple clusters and environments. Needs safe editing, clear topology summaries, and a tablet experience that works during active operations.

## SRE Lead

Monitors cluster health, recent changes, and impact radius. Cares about auditability, guardrails, and clear high-signal summaries during incidents.
